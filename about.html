About

Abct est un site web dédié au partage de typographies réalisées en par des artistes outsider aux ateliers de LA S et ZONE-ART.

Proposer une typographie

Be part of the project by sending us fonts that you designed in collaboration with mentally disabled artists.
Read more about the selection criteria and conditions by clicking THIS LINK.

You can also send us your modifications of our fonts or examples of them in use. As they are all free and open-source, let your imagination take over and use them as you want.
